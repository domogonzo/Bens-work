Register for updates using the Product Compatibility and Download Center

To register for Rockwell Automation product updates such as firmware updates and patch roll-ups, go to the Product Compatibility and Download Center. Click the Subscriptions icon to subscribe to specific product notifications.

Register for Knowledgebase updates

The Rockwell Automation Knowledgebase provides additional information on the latest product updates and patch information.

  • To receive notifications about specific products, follow the instructions provided in Knowledgebase Document ID: PN27 - Subscribing to Product Safety Advisory (PSA) or Product Notice (PN) Notifications.
  • To receive an email notification when the list of updates and patches is updated, select Subscribe to Updates at the top of Knowledgebase Document ID: QA57432 - Master list of all available Patch TOCs.
